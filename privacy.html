Privacy Policy

Overview

Last updated: July 15, 2026.

When you browse this website, we do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information unless you choose to contact us through the contact form or newsletter signup. We may use privacy-focused analytics to understand which content is most helpful. These tools do not track you across the internet or sell your data. We also use a small local storage entry to remember your dark mode preference, which is stored only in your browser.

Articles may contain links to external websites, tools, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party websites and encourage you to review their policies.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect any information you provide. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact page or email hello@kirankadam.in.
